Lucy's Interlude
Experimental Video Edit, 1920x1080
This work of art was inspired by one of my best friends who composed his first original song. His creativity with audio inspired me to create a visual element of how I personally experienced the song that he created. His song put me into a trance of sorts so I decided to create a loop that captures this trance feeling that I experienced. I synced the effects I was using to the flow of the song and that is how I managed to create the loop.
